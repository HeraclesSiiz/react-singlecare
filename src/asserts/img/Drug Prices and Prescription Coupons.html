Prescription FAQs

What pharmacies accept our coupon?

Our pharmacy savings card is accepted nationwide at over 35,000 pharmacies, including CVS Pharmacy®, Target, Walgreens, Walmart, Kroger, Albertsons, Fry's, Harris Teeter, Duane Reade, Longs Drugs and their respective subsidiaries. Simply bring your coupon to the pharmacy and ask the pharmacist to process your prescription using the BIN and PCN number found on your coupon.

To look up a drug price or to see if your pharmacy accepts our coupon, search for your prescription at the top of this page.

Can I receive savings on over-the-counter medications?

Virtual Me's prescription benefit only provide savings on prescription medications. Some over-the-counter drugs, such as Advil, have stronger forms that require a prescription, and that form of prescription may have a savings.
